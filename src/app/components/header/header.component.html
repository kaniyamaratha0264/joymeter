What is JoyMeter?

Learn more >>

JoyMeter enables agile teams to provide fast, direct and thus immediately actionable feedback within the team.

With JoyMeter, Agilibo has created a virtual solution for the Happiness Door concept of Management 3.0.

With just a few clicks, team members can provide brief feedback after retros, reviews and other business meetings on how happy and satisfied they were with the meetings.

JoyMeter thus promotes not only open, honest feedback in the team, but also cohesion and happiness among team members.
